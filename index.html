Welcome to PublicSent

This python based tool allows individuals, businesses and organizations to check how well (or badly) their product or service is perceived by the public. It does this by searching the web among other social media platforms and finds the search query. The tool uses the publicly available data to calculate the sentiment on a topic of interest.
